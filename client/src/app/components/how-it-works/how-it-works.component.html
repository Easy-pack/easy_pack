How It Works

1

Make An Order

Signup if you do not have an account or just Login if you do. Go to add transaction and fill the transaction form information

  • Simple use
  • No need to go to your transporter
  • No more hassle with phone calls
2

Make A Payment

Proceed to the payment securely. you just have to fill your payments information and Easy pack will pay the delivery men/women after the transaction is completed

  • Secure interface
  • Easy to use
3

Track Your Order

finally, just relax and track your order from the Easy pack tracking Interface

  • See if your package id delivered
  • Track all of your transactions
  • Rate your delivery men/women
